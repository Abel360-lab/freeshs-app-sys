About Log Cleanup

Why cleanup logs? Audit logs can grow very large over time, affecting system performance and storage requirements. Regular cleanup helps maintain optimal system performance.

What gets deleted? Only routine audit logs older than the specified retention period will be deleted. Critical security events are always preserved.

When to cleanup? Consider running cleanup monthly or quarterly, depending on your system's activity level and storage constraints.

Backup recommendation: Before running cleanup, consider exporting important logs to an external system for long-term archival.
